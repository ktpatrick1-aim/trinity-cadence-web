The Trinity Framework

Six Pillars. One Cadence.

Vision

The Blueprint captures your True North, Horizon (3-year picture), Vista (1-year view), and Year Ahead targets — all on one page.

People

Seat Map visualizes your org chart. Fit Check evaluates every seat with Grasp, Drive, and Capacity — replacing subjective reviews with structured clarity.

Data

Pulse tracks 5-15 Signals weekly across a 13-week rolling window. AI anomaly detection flags trends before they become problems.

Issues

The Dock captures everything. The Forge Loop (Surface, Shape, Solve) drives resolution. AI clustering groups related items when 8+ accumulate.

Process

Playbooks document your core processes with 16 industry-grounded templates drawn from ITIL, SCOR, PMI, and SHRM frameworks.

Progress

Anchors set 90-day priorities. Next Seven tracks weekly to-dos. Huddle runs your weekly leadership meeting with a built-in agenda.
