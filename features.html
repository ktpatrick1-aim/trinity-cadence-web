The Trinity Framework

Six Pillars. One Cadence.

Everything your leadership team needs to set the vision, build the structure, track the data, solve the issues, document the process, and measure the progress — in one AI-native operating system.

Vision Pillar

The Blueprint

Your single-page strategic document that captures everything that matters — from your company's purpose to this year's plan. The Blueprint replaces scattered slide decks and forgotten strategy docs with one living document your leadership team actually references every week.

AI Blueprint Coaching prompts you when sections feel generic or incomplete, pushing your team toward specificity and differentiation.

  • ✓ True North — Purpose, Cause, Passion + your Niche
  • ✓ Horizon — 10-year target
  • ✓ Vista — 3-year picture
  • ✓ Year Ahead — 1-year plan
  • ✓ Marketing Strategy — Target Market, Three Uniques, Proven Process, Guarantee
  • ✓ AI Blueprint Coaching — Prompts when sections feel generic (Pro)
People Pillar

The Seat Map & The Fit Check

The Seat Map is your drag-and-drop org structure that defines every seat in the company with clear accountability. No more ambiguity about who owns what. The Fit Check evaluates every person against Grasp, Drive, and Capacity — the three qualities that determine whether someone truly fits their seat.

  • ✓ Seat Definitions — Roles and measurables for every position
  • ✓ Team Hierarchy — One level of nesting for clean accountability
  • ✓ Role Assignments — Owner, Visionary, Operator, Leadership, Manager, Employee
  • ✓ Fit Check — Grasp, Drive, Capacity evaluation for every seat
  • ✓ Drag-and-Drop — Restructure your org visually in seconds
Data Pillar

The Pulse

Your weekly scorecard, per team. The Pulse tracks the 5-15 Signals that tell you whether your business is healthy — without waiting for month-end financials. A 13-week rolling trend view gives you the pattern, not just the snapshot.

AI anomaly detection flags Signals that have trended off-track for 3+ consecutive weeks, creating Dock items before small problems become crises.

  • ✓ 5-15 Signals — Per team, weekly
  • ✓ 13-Week Rolling Trend — See the pattern, not just the number
  • ✓ Red / Yellow / Green Thresholds — Instant visual status
  • ✓ Direction Settings — Higher is better, lower is better, within range
  • ✓ AI Anomaly Detection — Flags 3+ week trends automatically
Issues Pillar

The Dock & The Forge Loop

The Dock is your team's issues list with priority voting — a single place to capture every obstacle, idea, and concern. The Forge Loop walks your team through a disciplined resolution workflow: Surface the real issue, Shape the solution, and Solve with a clear action.

When your Dock hits 8+ items, AI issue clustering identifies patterns and groups related items — so you fix 1 root cause instead of playing whack-a-mole with 5 symptoms.

  • ✓ Upvote Prioritization — Team votes surface what matters most
  • ✓ Drag-to-Reorder — Manual priority override when needed
  • ✓ Forge Loop — Surface → Shape → Solve workflow
  • ✓ AI Issue Clustering — Groups related items at 8+ issues
  • ✓ Resolution Routing — Converts solved issues to Next Seven items, Anchors, or Playbook updates
Process Pillar

The Playbooks

Your documented core processes — not dusty binders, but living Playbooks that your team actually follows. Start with 16 industry-grounded templates built on recognized frameworks, then customize to match how your company actually operates.

Lived By All adherence tracking ensures your documented processes aren't shelf-ware. AI Playbook Refiner (Pro) analyzes your Playbooks and suggests improvements based on industry best practices.

  • ✓ 16 Industry-Grounded Templates — Ready to customize
  • ✓ Framework Citations — ITIL 4, SCOR, PMI PMBOK, SHRM, APQC, ASCM
  • ✓ Lived By All Tracking — Adherence monitoring across teams
  • ✓ AI Playbook Refiner — Suggests improvements based on best practices (Pro)
Progress Pillar

Anchors, The Next Seven & The Huddle

Anchors are your 90-day priorities at company, team, and individual levels — the bridge between your annual plan and weekly execution. The Next Seven is your 7-day to-do list that captures every commitment made during the Huddle. The Huddle is your weekly meeting with a timed agenda that keeps your team focused and accountable.

  • ✓ Anchors — 90-day priorities at company, team, and individual levels
  • ✓ Milestone Tracking — Break Anchors into measurable milestones
  • ✓ Status Tracking — On-track, at-risk, off-track, done
  • ✓ Rollup Reporting — See progress across all levels
  • ✓ The Next Seven — 7-day to-do list with ownership
  • ✓ The Huddle — Weekly meeting with timed agenda
  • ✓ Huddle Agenda — Segue → Pulse Review → Anchor Review → Headlines → Next Seven Review → Forge Loop → Conclude (1-10 rating)
  • ✓ AI Anchor SMART-ification — Rewrites vague priorities into SMART format (Pro)
  • ✓ AI Huddle Summary — Auto-generates summary + extracts Next Seven items (Pro)
